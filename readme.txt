== Description ==
<blockquote>That I believe if life gives you lemons, make lemonade ... You should try to find somebody And Whose Life has Given them vodka, and have a party.</blockquote>
<br />
It is a tool of social networks to counter your post. 

<strong>It has the following counters </strong>
<ul> 
<li> Facebook </li> 
<li> Twitter </li> 
<li> Google Plus </li> 
</ul> 
<h3>Position  counter</h3> 
You can display the counters in both the top and bottom of the content in your Post. 
It is a lightweight plugin and works fine with new features.
-You can also edit the button text




Gives shape and life to your post with 'Social Share Pop' ;)

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Social Share Pop'
3. Activate 'Social Share Pop' from your Plugins page.
4. Visit 'Setting > 'Social Share Pop'.
